Nachos

Description

Nachos are a highly customizable dish and can be tailored to anyone's particular toast. This recipe is for Loaded Nachos .

This dish is wonderfully filling and can be shared by a party of up to 6 people.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Sliced Olives
  • Tomatoes
  • Jalapenos

Steps

  1. Place the tortilla chips in an even layer over the baking sheet.
  2. Sprinkle half of the available shredded cheddar evenly across the chips.
  3. Finely dice the tomatoes and jalapenos, as well as the olives if you do not have pre-sliced ones.
  4. Evenly distribute the toppings across the tortilla chips.
  5. Cover the dish with the remaining half of your shredded cheese.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees Fahreheit for 10-15 minutes.
